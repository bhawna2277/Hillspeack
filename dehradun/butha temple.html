Budha Temple

Famous for extravagant Buddhist structure and holy school of Tibetan religion, Buddha Temple is the most visited place in Dehradun city. Located at Clement Town, Buddha Temple is center of attraction for many International tourists and Budhhist followers. Built-in the later years of the 20th century by the revered Buddhist religious head Kochen Rinpoche, Buddha Temple in Dehradun is a magnificent Tibetan Monastery and is also known as Mindrolling Monastery. It was built not only to safeguard the religious and cultural sentiments of the Buddhists but also to promote the same. The Temple is essentially a built-in Japanese architecture style on a highland in the Doon Valley. The view from the top is simply mesmerizing. If you wish to enjoy complete greenery, you must visit Dehradun Buddhist Temple. Around the Buddha Temple in Dehradun, you will also find a number of shops, restaurants, and other public places. You may take a walk and explore these places on your own when you are here. There is also a public book shop here where you will find that most of the books are written in the Tibetan language.
